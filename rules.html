The Rules:

  1. Must be holding spork in your hand
  2. Must have more than 50% of spork for it to count (if broken)
  3. No replacement sporks
  4. Can't spork somebody while they're at their job/practices/performances
  5. Can't spork somebody while they're driving- anytime the car is not in park
    • not at red lights either
  6. Can't attach the spork to yourself
  7. Can't go into somebody's home to spork them without their parents' approval
  8. Respect spork free zones
  9. Don't hurt people when you spork them
  10. you also can not steal someone's spork or knock it out of someone's hand!
